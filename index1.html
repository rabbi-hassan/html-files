Teacher Training

Our 200 hour teacher training is for the person seeking a comprehensive understanding of yoga and the ability to teach. For the person who wants to deepen their own practice, even if they don't intend to teach.

FORMAT

  • OPTION 1: 6 x 20 hour video sections. Each section is complete with a quiz or assignment and a live discussion with Patty.
  • OPTION 2:  4 day in person immersion in Brisbane or Byron Bay led by Patty or attend one of our 4 day yoga retreats in Australia.
    *If you can't make the immersion or would prefer to complete the training online you can opt for 8 x 20 hour video sections. 

INVESTMENT

Learning yoga is a lifelong journey. Our trainings will deepen your personal practice and provide you with the skills to become a full time teacher or teach a few classes on the side. This course certifies you with Yoga Alliance to become a yoga teacher. 

WHY CHOOSE MANA®?

At Mana we extend our values of providing you with an experience to enrich your life for years to come. As a Mana trained teacher you will deepen your own understanding and develop a dedicated daily asana and meditation practice. You'll understand the traditional + modern teachings + the significance of the 8 limbs of yoga through practice, lecture, discussion, teaching and personal guidance from your teachers. 
This training is led by senior teacher Patty Perlman along with the assistance of skilled and talented teachers. You can take this course 100% online or opt for an online and in person hybrid.

Your Teachers

Patty Perlman

Patty is a senior teacher + the lead teacher for all Mana 200hr trainings. Patty is RYS 500 + ERYT 500. Patty has been practicing yoga for 17 years and teaching for 12 years. She is the founder of Mana Yoga Retreats, leads over 96 retreat guests per year, creates corporate programs and works exclusively with private clients. Her love for yoga and the great outdoors creates an authentic and down to earth training for the modern yogi.

Martin Collyer

Martin is a long term movement and yoga enthusiast.  His yoga practice spans almost two decades, his diligent practice and exploration of movement and artistry around three decades.  This passion led Marty to study physiotherapy full time at the University of Queensland, which he began in 2019.  He is passionate about effectively using yoga in all its facets to explore what it means to be most deeply, fully and exquisitely in this project of being human.  Moreover, he is deeply engaged in guiding new and established yoga teachers to find the most elegant expression of their work.  

Shae Gredy

Shae is a proud graduate from Mana Yoga’s first class of teacher trainees - Vinyasa and Yin. Shae found her authentic teaching voice, influenced by a combination of shared knowledge, ancient wisdom and her own personal experiences and realizations through the practice of self reflection, acceptance and understanding.
